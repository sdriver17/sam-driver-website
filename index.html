BIO

I am a third-year college student at Wesleyan University, pursuing a double major in computer science and theater. In the theater I have extensive experience as an actor, including training at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts, as well as in all facets of technical theater, though I specialize in lighting design.

As a computer scientist I have extensive experience coding in many prominent languages including Java, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, C, C++, and SML. Throughout my time at Wesleyan I have gained experience in algorithm design, programming language design, and functional programming as a whole.

In addition to my educational pursuits, I am a member of the Briarcliff Manor Volunteer Fire Department. My work for the BMFD has provided me with a keen eye for details and extensive collabarative work experience.
